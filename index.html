About Us

Yadidya Musicals offers professional guitar and keyboard lessons for children and adults in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Guided by faith and passion for music, the school provides both in-home and online classes, bringing quality music education to every learner.

Our mission is to equip young musicians with the skills, confidence, and joy needed to express themselves through music. We believe every student has unique potential, and our personalized approach helps unlock that musical journey.
