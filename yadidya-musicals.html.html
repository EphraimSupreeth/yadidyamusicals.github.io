About Us

Founded in 2024 by Mr. Samuel Yadidya, Yadidya Musicals offers professional guitar and keyboard lessons for children and adults in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Guided by faith and passion for music, the school provides both in-home and online classes, bringing quality music education to every learner.
